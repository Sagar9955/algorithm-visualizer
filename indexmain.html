Our Vision
Our Vision is to revolutionize the way students learn fundamental computing concepts by providing an interactive and intuitive platform. With our web application, we aim to make learning sorting algorithms engaging and accessible to learners of all levels. By offering visual representations of seven commonly used sorting techniques, along with customizable controls for speed and graph size, we empower users to explore and understand these concepts at their own pace. Our ultimate goal is to inspire curiosity and foster a deeper understanding of computational thinking among students, laying a strong foundation for their future endeavors in computer science and beyond."

FAQ

Common questions

Here are some of the most common questions that we get.
How do I sign up as a user? To sign up as a user, click on the 'Sign Up' button on the homepage and fill out the required information.
Can I switch between user, teacher, and admin roles? No, once you have signed up with a specific role, you cannot switch to another role. You would need to create a new account with the desired role.
What can teachers do on SORTIFY? Teachers can create and manage courses, track student progress, and interact with students through messaging and feedback.
How can I reset my password? To reset your password, click on the 'Forgot Password' link on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your registered email.
How secure is my data on SORTIFY? SORTIFY takes data security seriously and uses encryption protocols to protect user information. Your data is safe and secure on our platform.
